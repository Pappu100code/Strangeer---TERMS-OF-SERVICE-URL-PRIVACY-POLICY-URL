Last Updated: 2026-01-25

Welcome to **Krazy.xyz**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our Discord bot and related services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Krazy.xyz may collect and store the following information:

- Discord User ID
- Discord Server (Guild) ID
- Saved Litecoin (LTC) wallet addresses
- Saved UPI IDs
- Command usage data (for functionality and debugging)

We do **not** collect:
- Passwords
- Emails
- Phone numbers
- IP addresses
- Personal identity information

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data strictly to:
- Provide bot features and commands
- Store user-configured settings (LTC / UPI)
- Improve performance and reliability
- Prevent abuse and misuse

Your data is **never sold, shared, or rented** to third parties.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- Data is stored securely and accessed only when required for bot functionality.
- Reasonable measures are taken to protect against unauthorized access or misuse.
- No system is 100% secure, but we strive to keep your data safe.

---

## 4. Data Removal

You can remove your stored data at any time using:
- `/ltc remove`
- `/upi remove`

If you want all your data deleted, contact the bot developer or support team.
